Recovered Note

The drive was wiped clean. No contacts. No history. No messages. Only this text document remained.

“Heads always tilt upward in the right place. Millions visit, but no one notices the watcher.”

He believed secrets should never be stored on servers. He said servers are seized. Buildings are not.

An iron giant touches the clouds, painted by light every night. People remember its height, but forget the silent eye nearby.

He warned me:

“If you stand where the visitors stand, you will miss it. You must stand where the cameras stand.”

He left nothing else. No coordinates. Only observation.
